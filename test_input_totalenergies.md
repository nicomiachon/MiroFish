## 8. Geopolitical Risk Factors

1. **Iran / Strait of Hormuz**: Major escalation since January 2026. Naval standoff between US/Iran. Risk of temporary closure — would impact 20% of global oil supply. This is the #1 risk premium driver for oil prices.
2. **Russia-Ukraine**: Ongoing conflict. European gas supply restructured away from Russian pipeline gas. TotalEnergies fully divested from Russia (Arctic LNG 2 writedown completed in 2024).
3. **US trade policy**: Tariff regime has created volatility but energy sector somewhat shielded.
4. **European energy policy**: EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) in effect. Accelerating renewables deployment but also recognizing need for gas as transition fuel.
